Build a CashYar mobile-first finance app for Saudi university students.

CashYar is NOT a traditional budgeting app. It teaches financial literacy through BEHAVIORAL LEARNING — the app analyzes spending patterns and gives personalized AI insights after each transaction, helping students understand the impact of their daily financial decisions.

## Database (Supabase - already connected)

Use these 3 tables:

### users (5,000 students)
- user_id, uuid, age, gender, city, university, major, study_year
- living_status, scholarship, part_time_income, other_income, total_income
- monthly_budget, financial_goal, goal_amount, target_months, financial_personality

### transactions (~500,000 rows)
- transaction_id, user_id, datetime, category, merchant, amount
- payment_method, planned_purchase, online_purchase, purchase_location
- season, event, day_of_week, hour
- remaining_budget, monthly_spending, monthly_saving, goal_progress
- financial_score, financial_risk, recommended_action

Categories: Coffee, Restaurants, Groceries, Shopping, Transportation, Electronics, Entertainment, Education, etc.
Merchants: Starbucks, AlBaik, Jarir, Amazon, Uber, etc.

### behavioral_summary (~60,000 monthly rows)
- user_id, month, total_income, total_spending, total_saving, saving_rate
- restaurant_ratio, coffee_ratio, shopping_ratio, transport_ratio
- budget_adherence, financial_score, goal_progress, spending_trend
- AI labels: financial_risk (Low/Medium/High), will_reach_goal (Yes/No)
- behavior_label (Disciplined/Balanced/Impulsive/Overspender/Recovering)
- recommended_action (Reduce Coffee Spending, Increase Savings, etc.)

## App Screens

1. **Onboarding** — user selects financial goal (Laptop, Phone, Travel, Car...) and enters income sources
2. **Dashboard** — monthly budget ring, goal progress bar, financial score (0-100), risk level badge
3. **Transactions** — list with category icons, merchant names, amounts in SAR
4. **AI Insights** — personalized messages like:
   - "You spent 35% of your budget on restaurants. Reducing by 15% helps you reach your goal 1 month earlier."
   - "Great week! You saved 15% more than last week."
5. **Behavior Chart** — spending by category pie chart, monthly trend line
6. **Leaderboard** — gamification: best savers, longest budget streak (NOT highest spender)
7. **Recommendations** — show recommended_action from AI labels

## Design
- Modern fintech UI, RTL Arabic support
- Colors: green for savings, amber for warnings, red for overspending
- Saudi context: SAR currency, local merchants (AlBaik, Jarir, STC Pay)
- Dark mode support

## Tech
- React + TypeScript + Tailwind + shadcn/ui
- Supabase client for all data queries
- Recharts for visualizations
- Filter transactions by user_id (demo: user_id = 1)

## Key User Flow
Learn → Plan → Spend → Get Smart Insight → Improve Behavior → Reach Financial Goal

The app learns from BEHAVIOR not from lessons. Every transaction should trigger a contextual insight.
